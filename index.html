Who We Are

Eco Alliance Green Solutions is a Social Enterprise established on 20 January 2021 with a strong commitment to protecting the environment, promoting sustainable development, and empowering communities.

The organization was founded out of a deep concern for the growing environmental challenges facing our planet, including deforestation, pollution, climate change, and the loss of biodiversity. Our vision is to create a world where people and nature live in harmony, with communities that are environmentally conscious, economically empowered, and resilient to the impacts of climate change.
